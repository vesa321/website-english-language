Simple Present

I eat breakfast every morning.

Usage: Regular habits, facts, universal truths.

Present Continuous

She is studying for her exams.

Usage: Actions happening right now or temporary situations.

Present Perfect

They have finished their homework.

Usage: Actions completed recently or affecting the present.

Present Perfect Continuous

We have been waiting for an hour.

Usage: Actions that started in the past and continue to now.
